How to Deal with Phishing Attempts:

  • Check the website URL carefully and ensure it's secure (HTTPS).
  • Don't click links in suspicious emails or messages.
  • Use two-factor authentication (2FA) where possible.
  • Never share passwords or financial info unless you're 100% sure.
  • Contact your IT/security team if unsure.
  • Keep your systems and antivirus updated.
  • Real companies don’t ask for passwords via email.
